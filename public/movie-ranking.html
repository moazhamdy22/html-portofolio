The Best Movies According to Moaz

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Pursuit of Happyness

an inspiring biographical drama that follows Chris Gardner's journey from homelessness to successful entrepreneur, showcasing the power of perseverance and fatherly love.

Interstellar

visually stunning sci-fi epic that explores love, sacrifice, and humanity's quest for survival through a journey across the cosmos.

Home Alone

a beloved holiday comedy about a clever young boy who defends his home from bumbling burglars after accidentally being left behind by his family during Christmas.
